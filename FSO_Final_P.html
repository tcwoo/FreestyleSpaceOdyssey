~~~~~~~~~Freestyle Space Odyssey~~~~~~~~~


Use the Up, Down, Left, and Right arrows to move.

Use the Spacebar to Shoot.

Press "Q" to Quit. Press "R" to Restart.

Objective: Shoot the enemy ship to gain points.

Gain enough points to go up in level.

*Careful, the enemy may speed up!*

Unlock parts of a story as you travel!



The universe is ever expanding.

One day it will stop the growth and begin to shrink,

but by then I hope to be dead.

If you are reading this, then my hopes may be fulfilled.

I, in the sense of individualism, do not matter.

My name is not important, but my story is a different matter.



Species, planets, galaxies from various corners of the universe.

As sentient beings, our mind is constantly changing and evolving.

But I believe it is my deal with Time

that allows me to understand what I have seen.



I met Time at the same moment that I was introduced to Death.

They are both more complex entities than I expected.

I was stranded on Lysithea with my life support failing.

How insignificant a place in space and time?

Yet, this is where I learned of destiny.



Death appeared a step from my face.

She glared at me with cold, white eyes.

I saw the apparent insignificance of my existence in her eyes.

Then, a mirage of beautiful hues appeared next to her.

Death rolled her eyes as if this was routine as well.

Except when a tall dark man emerged from the mirage.



Death seemed to be shocked by this particular man’s presence.

They conversed for what seemed hours and yet it could not be.

The man said his name was Time.

He had such piercing, emerald eyes that showed

the longevity of the universe’s existence.

He gave me an offer that I could not in good conscience ignore.

Now I could tell you my task but that isn’t necessary.

You have been chosen to learn this objective.



I cannot die. Correction: I can die but not stay dead.

When I die, I am restored to a moment in time that I am alive.

Death and I have cultivated an interesting relationship.

I am the first organism that is not divine that has seen her

more than once without being dead.



The second time I saw her was during a smuggle run

through the Epsilon Eridani solar system.

The third was a coup d’etat on the planet, 91 Aquarii Ab,

about 230 years after. Dying isn’t fun,

but having a backup plan for failures is a gift.



This gift made me bold at first.

Danger was of no importance to me

Money was a quick finesse of royalty or banks away at any time.

But, as you have probably heard before,

Time catches up to all of us.

We had a long conversation about my purpose,

when suddenly, I had an epiphany.



A chance of a trillion lifetimes was right in front of me.

And I was wasting it. So I set off on a great journey.

This journey was one for knowledge.

For witnessing and understanding cultures, species and events

throughout the galaxies. I studied civilizations and I met

powerful organisms that were considered gods to billions.



Men like me have amassed much knowledge in the vast universe.

Yes, I am not the only one through history.

I and the others were tasked with two things

The first is to accomplish all your greatest desires.

The second is to record it for future generations

If you want to add to these records, then follow this step.



Smile, turn around, and look into those green eyes.
